How to Play Rock Paper Scissors

Simple and Entertaining Game

Rock Paper Scissors is a classic hand game usually played between two people. The game has three possible outcomes: a draw, a win, or a loss. Here's how it works:

  • Rock crushes scissors, so if you play rock and your opponent plays scissors, you win.
  • Paper covers rock, so if you play paper and your opponent plays rock, you win.
  • Scissors cut paper, so if you play scissors and your opponent plays paper, you win.
  • If both players choose the same item, the game is a draw and is usually played again.
  • It's a game of chance and quick thinking, often used to settle disputes or simply for entertainment.
